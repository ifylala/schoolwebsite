Admissions.

Stage 1

The first point of contact is the Admin Officer who will receive prospective parents, answer enquiries and give general information about the school.

Stage 2

An evident statement of result from the previous school will be required from the prospective parent, before purchase of admission form for ₦5000.

Stage 3

Parent completes the application form and submits it with all requested documentation to Admissions Officer who thereafter schedules an assessment date with the Principal.

Stage 4

King's Seed High School’s Entrance Assessment begins in March. Other assessments take place all year round depending on available openings.

Stage 5

The Admin Officer communicates to successful candidates and informs them of scheduled interview dates as determined by the Principal.

Stage 6

The Admin Officer sends messages to all candidates informing them of the outcome of the interview. Successful candidates are asked to pick up their admission letters.

Stage 7

As soon as school fees are paid, receipts are issued accordingly.

Note

The students can only start school after the full payment of the school fees is made.
