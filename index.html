We are skilled

KSHS is an educational institution committed to training and developing young leaders for the future. Our robust curriculum is uniquely-crafted to nurture independent and leadership traits in our students.

King’s Seed High School

As an institution for learning and development, we encourage students to develop the fundamental skills necessary for independent learning, problem solving and critical thinking. Our warm, caring and safe learning environment is designed to stimulate curiosity, freedom of expression, fairness and respect for others.

With advancing methods of teaching and learning, we use enhancing technology to deliver quality and effective lessons required to prepare students for today’s changing world.

2009

During the summer of the year 2009, the vision for King’s Seed High School was born and thereafter, founded on the 28th of September, 2009. With an opening session of just 20 students, KSHS has proceeded to accommodating over 500 students till date.

2014

The first ever graduating class of King’s Seed High School had their Valedictory service on the 21st of July, 2014. KSHS has since then developed an Alumni network of over 250 students till date.

2019

KSHS celebrated her 10-year milestone of delivering educational excellence.

Welcome Speech From The Principal

It is with great pleasure that I welcome you to King’s Seed High School. The school is committed to developing students who are purpose-driven, hence our motto…..purpose and excellence. King’s Seed High School is different by design. The School is a place to be, a place to learn, and a place to love as one body. We are a family school, which means we value the things, which make each of us distinctively brilliant.

King’s Seed High School is a Christian faith-based school, but we are open to families of all faiths who identify with our core values of godliness, intelligence, vision and excellence. Students are encouraged to push themselves academically while constantly focusing on the school’s core values.

Godliness dictates all that we do as a school and this has immensely helped us in developing disciplined, well-cultured and morally sound students.
